2. Introduction

Mentor Connect is an online platform that connects students with experienced mentors. It helps students receive guidance for career planning, skill development, internships, placements, and higher studies.

3. Problem Statement

Many students find it difficult to identify the right mentor for career and academic guidance. There is no single platform where students can easily connect with mentors and schedule mentoring sessions.

4. Objective

The main objective of this project is to connect students with suitable mentors through a centralized online platform and provide easy access to career and skill guidance.

5. Proposed Solution

The system allows students and mentors to register and log in. Students can search for mentors based on their interests, send mentoring requests, book sessions, and receive guidance. Mentors can manage requests and interact with students.

6. Modules

Student Module

- Registration & Login
- Search Mentor
- Book Session
- View Booking History
- Feedback

Mentor Module

- Registration & Login
- Manage Profile
- Accept/Reject Requests
- View Student Requests

Admin Module

- Manage Students
- Manage Mentors
- Monitor the Platform

9. Expected Outcome

Students can easily find suitable mentors and receive proper career guidance. The platform makes mentoring simple, organized, and accessible.

10. Future Enhancements

11. Conclusion

Mentor Connect provides a simple and effective solution to bridge the gap between students and mentors. It supports students in making better career decisions through timely guidance and mentorship.
